Understanding the Natural Language Processing (NLP) Power

Natural language processing (NLP) is a subfield of linguistics, computer science, information engineering, and artificial intelligence concerned with the interactions between computers and human (natural) languages, in particular how to program computers to process and analyze large amounts of natural language data.

Here we will see a small demonstration of the power of the web. If we think about what is possible to do with all the information exposed on the web, we can quickly understand that it can help us to interpret patterns that in turn will help us to better understand society as well as the individual. This is extremely important to understand trends such as diseases, for example coronavirus.
